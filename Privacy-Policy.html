Privacy Policy

Who is responsible for your data?

Van der Volpi
You can find all the ways to contact us on the contact page.

What personal data do we collect?

We only collect personal data when you:

  • Contact us via the website
  • Send us an email
  • Book a call

The data we collect may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Any information you choose to share in your message

Why do we collect this data?

We use your data only to:

  • Reply to your message
  • Communicate with you
  • Discuss potential services

Legal basis

We process your data based on:

  • Your consent
  • Our legitimate interest in responding to inquiries

How long do we keep your data?

We store your information to manage your requests and assist with any future questions. We generally keep your data for up to 3 years, but only as long as necessary, and you can ask us to have it deleted at any time.

Who has access to your data?

Your data is not sold or shared with third parties, unless required by law or necessary to run the website (e.g. Nicepage deals with our form submissions).

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct your data
  • Request deletion
  • Object to processing

You can exercise these rights by contacting: info@vandervolpi.com

Complaints

If you believe your data is handled incorrectly, you may file a complaint with the Belgian Data Protection Authority.
