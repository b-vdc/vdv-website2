Why I do this

By profession I'm an IP attorney. By passion I'm the creator behind @italiansinbelgium. So when we talk about a collab gone wrong or a repost without credit, I'm not translating from theory — I've drafted the contracts and posted the content.

I work with businesses, agencies and creators through workshops, trainings and hands-on legal support. The goal is always the same: your team makes smarter, safer decisions about content, collaborations and campaigns — and avoids the expensive mistakes.

What I stand for

Both sides of the table

I know what it feels like to launch a campaign, sign a collab and post the content. That dual perspective means advice that fits reality, not just theory.

Clarity
