Intellectual property

Create boldly. Protect wisely.

Your name, your designs, your content — protecting them shouldn't come as an afterthought. I help you build protection into the way you work.

For brands

  • Trademark and design strategy: what to register, where and when
  • IP guidance for your social media brand strategy
  • Content usage rights in creator collaborations
  • Spotting and reducing IP risks before a campaign launches

For creators

  • The IP risks hiding in music, memes and AI tools
  • Licensing support for brand deals
  • Keeping ownership of your work in collaboration agreements

Contracts

A clear contract today avoids conflict tomorrow.

Most legal problems on social media blow up because nobody clarified the rules upfront. A good contract is that clarification — in writing.

For brands

  • IP, confidentiality and license agreements, drafted or reviewed
  • Creator and influencer collaboration contracts
  • Clear definitions of content ownership and usage rights
  • Contract review and risk assessment before you sign

For creators

  • Support with brand and agency collaboration contracts
  • Guidance on content ownership and licensing terms
  • Negotiating fair terms — payment, deliverables, revisions
  • Understanding your obligations and exclusivity clauses

Social media

Creating content takes skill. Staying in control takes strategy.

Influencer marketing, brand-creator partnerships, reposting, disclosure tags — the everyday stuff of social media all has a legal side. Here's a few examples of how I can help.

For brands

  • Drafting and reviewing collaboration agreements
  • Defining who may use the content, where and for how long
  • Advertising compliance: tags and disclosures, including gifted products
  • Reposting and reusing content without stepping on someone's rights
  • Aligning your campaign setup with your brand strategy

For creators

  • Navigating disclosure rules — what to tag, when and how
  • Understanding what happens to your content after you post it
  • Negotiating agreements before you sign them
  • Knowing your licenses and usage rights inside out
